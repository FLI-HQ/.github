# FLI — Financial Learning & Intelligence

**Constitutionally constrained autonomous financial execution.**

FLI builds AI delegation infrastructure for people whose executive function is inconsistent, not absent. The system acts on behalf of users — monitoring accounts, detecting risk, executing protective actions — then explains what it did and why.

No confirmation flows. No engagement mechanics. No investment advice. The constitutional constraints are not a compliance layer. They are the product.

---

## Architecture

- **Runtime:** Python 3.13 · FastAPI · SQLAlchemy
- **AI layer:** Anthropic Claude API · constitutional decision framework
- **Banking:** Starling Bank webhooks · TrueLayer Open Banking (NatWest, Barclays)
- **Regulatory:** FCA Innovation Pathways · Supercharged Sandbox pathway · NICE Digital Therapeutics

---

## Governance

FLI operates under six hard constitutional prohibitions. These are enforced at the AI decision layer — not as policy, but as architectural constraint. Full framework: [`syndic-constitution`](https://github.com/FLI-HQ/syndic-constitution)
